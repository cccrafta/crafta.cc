## Angle
Multiple denim mills are independently arriving at the same conclusion: heritage textures and workwear silhouettes, executed in premium materials. 60% cashmere denim. Viscose pleated denim. Fully traceable cotton stories. This isn't nostalgia — it's the industry deciding that denim's blue-collar identity is now a luxury proposition. What does this mean for the people who actually wore it to work?
