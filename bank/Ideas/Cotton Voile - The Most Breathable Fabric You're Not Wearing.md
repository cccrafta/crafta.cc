## Angle
Featherweight, sheer, and built for brutal heat — the forgotten summer shirting. Cotton voile is a crisp, semi-sheer plain weave with an exceptionally high thread count relative to its weight, making it one of the most effective heat-beating fabrics that has quietly fallen out of mainstream awareness.
