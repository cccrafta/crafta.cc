## Angle
From Indian handlooms to Ivy League campuses — a pattern with more history than its wearers realize. Madras cloth originated in the city of Chennai (formerly Madras) as hand-woven, vegetable-dyed cotton, and its journey to American preppy staple is a story of colonial trade, cultural appropriation, and enduring craft.
