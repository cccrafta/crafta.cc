## Angle
Persian origins, puckered engineering, and the physics of air circulation in fabric. Seersucker's alternating smooth and puckered stripes create air pockets that hold the fabric away from the skin, enabling convective cooling that makes it one of the most functionally effective summer textiles ever devised.
