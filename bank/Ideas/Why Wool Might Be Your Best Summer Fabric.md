## Angle
Tropical fresco wool — the counterintuitive summer choice that outperforms cotton. High-twist, open-weave wools like fresco and tropical are naturally moisture-wicking, odor-resistant, and surprisingly breathable, making them a superior warm-weather option that most people overlook.
