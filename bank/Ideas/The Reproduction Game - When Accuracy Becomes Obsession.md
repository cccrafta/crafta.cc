## Angle
Brands like Warehouse and Buzz Rickson source deadstock fabric and original machinery to recreate garments stitch-for-stitch. The Japanese reproduction denim scene takes historical fidelity to extraordinary extremes, treating vintage garments as archaeological artifacts to be reverse-engineered down to the thread count, button alloy, and selvage line color.
