## Angle
Snap buttons, pointed yokes, and smile pockets — every detail of the Western shirt was designed for horseback. The Western shirt's distinctive features are not decorative affectations but functional innovations born from the demands of ranch work, each element solving a specific problem that buttons, flat seams, and straight pockets could not.
