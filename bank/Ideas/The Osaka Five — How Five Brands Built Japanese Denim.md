## Angle
The Osaka Five didn't just copy American denim — they elevated it into something the originators hadn't imagined. Each of the five took a different approach: Evisu went theatrical, Warehouse went archival, Fullcount went technical. The story of how five brands in one city created an entire industry is also a story about what happens when outsiders take a tradition more seriously than the culture that created it.
