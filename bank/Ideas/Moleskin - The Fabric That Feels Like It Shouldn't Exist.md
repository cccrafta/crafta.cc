## Angle
A cotton fabric brushed to feel like suede — heavier than flannel, softer than canvas, and nearly forgotten. Moleskin is a dense, heavily napped cotton that achieves a velvety hand-feel through aggressive brushing, and it was once the default workwear fabric across France and Britain before being displaced by synthetic alternatives.
