## Angle
A two-piece garment originally worn by Zen Buddhist monks for manual labor — now adopted by craftspeople worldwide. The samue consists of a wrapped top and drawstring pants designed for unrestricted movement during temple work, and its simple, functional construction has made it a favorite among potters, woodworkers, and artisans far beyond Japan.
