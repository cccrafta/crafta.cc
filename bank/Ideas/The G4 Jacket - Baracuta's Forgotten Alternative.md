## Research Notes
From Harrington research: G4 created 1944, button cuffs and open waist vs G9's elasticated. Adopted by American Ivy League college students as middle ground between casual and formal. Shared umbrella back yoke, Fraser tartan lining, dog ear collar with G9. Both adopted by same British subcultures (Mods, Skinheads, Punks).

## Angle
Design analysis of why the fitted blouson silhouette (G9) became culturally dominant over the relaxed fit (G4). Is it purely aesthetic preference, or did the elasticated cuffs/waist create functional advantages (warmth retention, clean lines when moving)? Explore the alternate timeline where G4 became the name everyone knows.
