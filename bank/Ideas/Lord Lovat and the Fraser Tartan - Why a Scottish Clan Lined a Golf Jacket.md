## Research Notes
From Harrington research: Fraser tartan has 13th-century Scottish-Gaelic roots. Lord Lovat (Simon Fraser) granted permission 1938. His WWII service as decorated commander added prestige to the tartan. Pattern and color unchanged since 1938. But no documentation of *why* tartan was chosen or how permission was negotiated.
