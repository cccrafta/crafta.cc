## Angle
The tuxedo has a specific job: make the wearer appropriate for a formal occasion while allowing them to move, eat, and socialise for hours. Every detail — silk facings, higher waistband, side adjusters, grosgrain stripe — solves a functional problem. It's not costume. It's equipment for a specific environment, the way a safari jacket is equipment for the bush.
