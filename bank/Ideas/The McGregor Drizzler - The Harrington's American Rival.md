## Research Notes
From Harrington research: McGregor released "Drizzler" 1947 as competing design with point collar instead of stand collar, no tartan lining. James Dean wore a McGregor (not Baracuta) in some appearances.

## Angle
Explore design decisions that determine iconic status vs also-ran status. Point collar vs stand collar, tartan lining vs plain — small details, huge cultural outcomes. What made Baracuta the name everyone knows?
