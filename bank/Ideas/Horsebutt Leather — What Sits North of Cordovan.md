## Angle
Everyone knows shell cordovan. But Horween's "North of Cordovan" leather — the horsebutt surrounding the shell — is less documented and rarely isolated as a premium material. Crown Northampton's 30-pair limited run is a chance to explore what this leather actually is, how it differs from shell, and why a maker would choose it.
