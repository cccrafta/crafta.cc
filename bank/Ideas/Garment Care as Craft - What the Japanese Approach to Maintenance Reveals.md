## Angle
Most garment writing ends at purchase. The relationship between a person and a well-made garment only really begins there. Japanese denim culture is obsessive about this: the Levi's 501 fade documented above (2.5 years, 14 soaks, 40 washes) is a care narrative as much as a wear narrative. Same with waxed cotton re-waxing, shell cordovan conditioning, Goodyear welt resoling. The craft isn't just in the making — it's in the maintaining. A technique post about what good care actually involves across different materials.
