## Angle
Cone Mills closed in 2017, but Vidalia Mills and others are keeping American selvedge alive. The closure of White Oak, America's last shuttle-loom denim plant, seemed like the end of domestic selvedge production, but a new generation of mills is proving that American-made selvedge denim still has a future.
