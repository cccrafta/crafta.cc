## Angle
Why some denim spirals around your leg and how a simple weave reversal solved a century-old problem. Traditional right-hand twill denim has a natural tendency to torque after washing, causing the leg seam to rotate; broken twill alternates the diagonal direction every few rows, canceling this twist while creating a distinctive surface texture.
