## Research Notes
From Harrington research: John Simons opened The Ivy Shop in Richmond 1964, placed hand-written felt-tip note in window 1965 dubbing G9 "The Rodney Harrington" after Peyton Place character. Six-decade career as definitive voice on Ivy League style in UK. Permanent Style has detailed profile (2021).

## Angle
The story of how retail marketing creates language. John Simons as cultural intermediary between American Ivy style and British Mods. His influence beyond just naming the jacket — what else did he introduce/popularize?
