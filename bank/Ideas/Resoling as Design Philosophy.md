## Angle
A shoe designed to be resoled is a different object than a shoe designed to be worn out. Crown Northampton publishing their resole process signals that repairability is now a marketing proposition, not just an engineering one. But the deeper story is how design-for-repair changes the original construction decisions — what gets stitched vs cemented, how soles attach, what materials are chosen for longevity vs initial comfort.
