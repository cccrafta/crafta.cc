## Angle
The Barbour jacket as a case study in design for longevity. 160 parts, 36 people per jacket, 13,000 re-waxed annually. Queen Elizabeth kept hers for 25 years. The Bedale was designed for riding, the Beaufort for shooting — each detail solves a specific problem. The jacket is not sold as a finished product but as the beginning of a relationship with a material that improves with maintenance.
