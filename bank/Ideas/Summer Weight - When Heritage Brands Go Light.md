## Angle
The trend of lightweight versions of heavy classics — 7.5oz corduroy, 12oz denim, linen overshirts. Heritage brands are increasingly reinterpreting their heavyweight staples in summer-friendly weights, proving that rugged character and warm-weather wearability are not mutually exclusive.
