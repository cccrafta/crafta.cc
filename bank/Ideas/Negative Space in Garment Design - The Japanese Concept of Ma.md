## Angle
How intentional emptiness and loose silhouettes in Japanese fashion reflect a deeper design philosophy. The concept of ma — the purposeful use of negative space and intervals — permeates Japanese garment design, where the space between body and fabric is not wasted but is itself the design, creating movement, breathing room, and visual quiet.
