## Angle
How a lab-created cellulose fiber became summer's most underrated shirt fabric. Rayon and its modern descendant Tencel are derived from wood pulp through chemical processing, yet they achieve a fluid drape and cool hand-feel that rivals silk at a fraction of the cost.
