## Angle
Unripe persimmon tannin creates a waterproof, antibacterial finish that darkens with sunlight over years. Kakishibu is a centuries-old Japanese dyeing technique where fermented persimmon juice is applied to fabric, producing a rich amber-brown that deepens with UV exposure, making each garment a living record of time spent in the sun.
