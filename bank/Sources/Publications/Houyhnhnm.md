## About
Japanese fashion and lifestyle web magazine. Covers fashion, culture, food, and lifestyle with a Tokyo-centric perspective.

## Editorial Angle
Lifestyle-integrated fashion. Covers brands, stores, events, and cultural moments. Strong on the intersection of fashion with other creative disciplines.

## Key Coverage Areas
- Japanese fashion brands and stores
- Vintage and heritage (New Vintage series)
- Lifestyle (food, travel, culture)
- Collaborations and limited editions

## Relevance to Crafta
Essential for Japanese perspective. Covers brands, stores, and cultural context that Western publications miss. Requires translation but provides unique insights.
