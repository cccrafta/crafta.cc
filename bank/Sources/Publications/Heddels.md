## About
Menswear publication focused on denim, workwear, and heritage brands. Known for detailed product reviews, brand profiles, and educational content about materials and construction.

## Editorial Angle
Highly detailed, product-focused. Strong on technical specifications (fabric weight, weave type, construction methods). Less opinion, more information. The go-to reference for denim and workwear specifics.

## Key Coverage Areas
- Denim (selvedge, raw, fading)
- Workwear (jackets, shirts, trousers)
- Heritage brands (Japanese and American)
- Materials (fabrics, leathers, dyeing techniques)
- Footwear (boots, heritage sneakers)
