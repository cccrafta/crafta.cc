## About
Publication focused on bags, carry goods, and the materials behind them. Covers waxed canvas, heritage bags, and the craft of making things that carry other things.

## Editorial Angle
Material-focused, practical. Strong on waxed canvas culture, bag construction, patina, and heritage brands. Reviews are detailed on materials and construction rather than just aesthetics.

## Key Coverage Areas
- Waxed canvas bags and culture
- Heritage carry brands (Filson, Tanner Goods, Billykirk)
- Material deep dives
- Bag construction and design

## Relevance to Crafta
Essential for Materials and Technique posts involving bags, canvas, and waxed cotton. Their patina coverage and material analysis aligns with Crafta's observation approach.
