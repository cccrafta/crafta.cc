## About
Founded 2007 by Simon Crompton, former journalist (Oxford PPE). Full-time since ~2018. Listed as one of the best men's sites by GQ, Esquire, The Times, NYT. Editorially independent — no advertising-for-coverage deals.

## Editorial Angle
First-person reviews based on years of using bespoke makers. Factory visits worldwide. Interviews with craftsmen and designers. Uniquely positioned: he commissions bespoke garments and reviews the process and result. Not a product reviewer — a craft journalist.

## Key Coverage
- Bespoke tailoring (Savile Row, Neapolitan, Japanese)
- Cloth and fabric (detailed analysis of weights, weaves, mills)
- Casual jacket taxonomy (safari, chore, teba, overshirt)
- Shoe and leather craft
- Brand reviews with multiple visits over years
