## About
Classic menswear blog. Covers traditional men's clothing with practical buying guides and style advice.

## Editorial Angle
Opinionated and practical. Strong on seasonal dressing guides, fabric recommendations, and thrift/vintage finds. More accessible than Heddels — writes for the curious dresser, not the denim obsessive.

## Key Coverage Areas
- Seasonal dressing guides (summer fabrics, winter layering)
- Fabric deep dives (rayon, linen, fresco wool)
- Style advice and wardrobe building
- Vintage and thrift shopping

## Relevance to Crafta
Excellent for fabric and material research. Their seasonal guides are well-sourced and practical. Good complement to Heddels' product focus.
