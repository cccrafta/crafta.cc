## Overview
Los Angeles outpost of the Nepenthes group, founded by Keizo Shimizu in Tokyo. The blog covers product releases, collaborations, and lifestyle editorial for the Nepenthes family of brands: Engineered Garments, Needles, South2 West8, and Nepenthes NY/LA house labels.

## Editorial Focus
- Engineered Garments seasonal drops and construction details
- Needles and South2 West8 product features
- "Nepenthes People" profiles — customers, musicians, creatives in the orbit
- Collaborations (Converse, AS2OV, etc.)
- Travel and lifestyle storytelling from the LA perspective

## Relevance to Crafta
T1 primary source — Nepenthes is a maker/retailer with direct knowledge of their garments. First-hand product descriptions, construction notes, and design intent from the people behind the brands. Valuable for Garments, Design, and Culture categories. The "Nepenthes People" series offers cultural context around who wears these clothes and why.
