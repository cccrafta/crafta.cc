## About
Iconic Japanese lifestyle magazine. "Magazine for City Boys." Covers fashion, culture, food, and urban life with a distinctive casual-cool aesthetic.

## Editorial Angle
Lifestyle-first. Fashion as part of a broader cultural identity. Strong on small Japanese brands, craft culture, and the intersection of clothing with daily life.

## Key Coverage Areas
- City boy lifestyle and culture
- Small Japanese brands
- Craft and artisan culture (indigo dyeing, ceramics)
- Seasonal style and wardrobe
- Watches and accessories

## Relevance to Crafta
Popeye's approach to fashion-as-lifestyle aligns well with Crafta's editorial voice. Good for discovering niche Japanese brands and cultural context that no Western publication covers.
