## About
The V&A is the world's leading museum of art and design, located in London. The Fashion collection spans four centuries and is the largest of its kind. Searchable online collection database with high-resolution images.

## Research Value
- **Collection database** — searchable by material, date, origin, technique
- **Fashion collection** — historical garments that can verify construction claims, date attributions, material usage
- **Textile collection** — fabric samples, weaving patterns, dyeing techniques

## How to Use
Search the collection at vam.ac.uk/collections for:
- Specific garment types (e.g. "safari jacket" to find surviving examples)
- Materials (e.g. "moleskin" to verify historical usage dates)
- Makers (e.g. "Barbour" to find archived examples)

## Relation to Crafta
T1 primary source for historical verification. When a post claims "moleskin was used by French workers in the 19th century," the V&A collection can potentially show a surviving example. The highest tier of evidence for historical claims.
