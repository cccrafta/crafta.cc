## About

London-based vintage clothing archive and showroom. Houses thousands of original garments spanning workwear, military, sportswear, and tailoring from the 1880s to the 1980s. Used by designers, stylists, and film productions as a reference resource. Their philosophy: "Holey, torn, faded, muddy, warped, or stained clothing can still hold beauty."

## Editorial Focus

- Curated exhibitions of archival garments organised by theme (film, culture, era)
- Garment history told through the pieces themselves
- Student and industry collaborations
- Behind-the-scenes of their archive

## Relevance to Crafta

T1 primary source — they own and curate the archive. When they describe a garment's provenance, construction, or history, it's first-hand documentation. Particularly valuable for posts about vintage workwear, military garments, and garment aging/patina.
