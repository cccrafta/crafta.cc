## About

Tokyo-based sustainable fashion brand founded by Austrian-Japanese designer. Specialises in made-to-order clothing using Japanese textiles — selvedge denim, kakishibu-dyed fabrics, Tencel, Alcantara. Zero-waste design philosophy. Collaborates with artisans (EDWIN denim, kimekomi craft) and shows at events like Dubai Design Week.

## Editorial Focus

- Japanese textile deep dives: selvedge denim production in Okayama, kakishibu dyeing, sustainable fibres
- Craft collaborations and exhibitions (MANA SPACE, The Goodlife Dubai)
- Maker perspective on material choices and construction
- Brand storytelling with embedded technical detail
