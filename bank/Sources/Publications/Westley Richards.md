## About
Birmingham gunmaker and outfitter, established 1812. Over 200 years of outfitting hunters and adventurers. Safari clothing designed for actual field use — direct lineage from the jackets built for Hemingway's generation.

## Key Products
- Selous Safari Jacket (signature piece, named after Frederick Courteney Selous)
- Bushveld Safari Jacket (100% cotton drill, designed for actual bush use)
- Campaign Safari Shirts
- Explorer bags and leather goods

## Editorial Perspective
Writes from the perspective of actual field use — their blog (The Explora) covers safari clothing history, client stories, and practical testing. Not fashion-driven; function-first with heritage authority.

## Relation to Crafta
Essential source for the "equipment design" angle of safari clothing — where function dictates form. Their Bushveld jacket's 100% cotton drill specification is a benchmark for authentic safari construction.
