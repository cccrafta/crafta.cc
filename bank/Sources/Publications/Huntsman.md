## About
Savile Row tailor known for structured, military-influenced silhouettes. Made bespoke safari suits for Clark Gable, Ava Gardner, and Grace Kelly for *Mogambo* (1953). Creative Director Campbell Carey.

## Safari Offerings
- Bespoke safari jackets
- Ready-to-wear: Seagreen Linen Herringbone Safari Jacket (softer construction)
- Current collection includes herringbone and seersucker options

## Editorial Perspective
Positions the safari jacket as evolved from utilitarian wear into refined "off-duty" style. More structured than most contemporary interpretations — retains the military heritage.

## Relation to Crafta
The Huntsman-Mogambo connection is the clearest documented case of a Savile Row house making safari jackets for a specific film. Contrasts with The Armoury/Bryceland's softer approach.
