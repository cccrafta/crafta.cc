## About
Dutch denim magazine. "Daily Magazine About Denim & Authentic Products." Covers denim culture, trade shows, brand stories, and collector culture.

## Editorial Angle
Community-focused, celebrates denim culture and the people behind it. Strong on trade show coverage (Kingpins) and collector stories.

## Key Coverage Areas
- Denim culture and community
- Trade shows and industry events
- Brand profiles
- Collector culture and vintage

## Relevance to Crafta
Good for cultural context and industry trends. Less technical than Heddels, more about the community and lifestyle around denim.
