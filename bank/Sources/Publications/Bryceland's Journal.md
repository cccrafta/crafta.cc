## Overview

Bryceland's is a menswear house with stores in Tokyo and Hong Kong, founded by Ethan Newton and Tony Sylvester. Their journal is among the best craft-focused menswear editorial in English — not a trade publication, not a style guide, but writing by people who genuinely care about how garments are made and why certain makers matter.

## Editorial Perspective

Coverage spans classic tailoring, American workwear, and what Bryceland's calls "leisure wear" — the space between the two. Articles tend to examine specific garments or makers in depth: construction details, material choices, the philosophy behind a particular workshop. Guest contributors include G. Bruce Boyer.
