## About
Menswear opinion blog. Covers street style, indie brands, and fashion culture with a personal editorial voice.

## Editorial Angle
Opinionated and reflective. Writes about fashion culture, trends, and the meaning behind how people dress. Less product-focused, more cultural commentary.

## Relevance to Crafta
Good for cultural angles and opinion-driven pieces. The editorial voice is similar to what Crafta aspires to — thoughtful, not promotional.
