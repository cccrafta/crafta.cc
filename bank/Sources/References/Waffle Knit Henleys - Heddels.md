## Summary
Roundup of premium waffle knit henleys. Covers how waffle knit creates air pockets for insulation.

## Key Claims
- Waffle knit creates small pockets that trap air for insulation
- Japanese makers (Iron Heart, UES, Loop & Weft, Warehouse) lead quality
- UES Big Waffle: 13oz Japanese waffle weave, true year-round garment
- Loop & Weft's double-faced thermal fabric was so difficult to manufacture the factory refused reorders
