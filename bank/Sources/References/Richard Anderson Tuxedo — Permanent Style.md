## Summary
Detailed construction analysis of a bespoke Savile Row tuxedo. Covers canvas structure (body canvas, horsehair, domette), lapel width, gorge position, shoulder construction, and handwork details.

## Key Claims
- Body canvas runs length of jacket, horsehair at top, domette over that
- Two darts on either side for waist shape
- Narrow shoulder finishing before outer edge of deltoid
- Grosgrain facing attached by hand
- Buttonholes slim, neat, handmade
