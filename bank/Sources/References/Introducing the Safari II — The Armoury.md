## Summary
The Safari II evolves The Armoury's decade-long safari jacket offering. Made by Ascot Chang (Hong Kong).

## Key Claims
- Safari jacket has been an Armoury essential for over a decade
- Safari II appears more minimal but has enhanced functionality
- Waist pockets: enlarged with both gussets and bellows, plus two internal change pockets
- Chest pockets: elongated, rounded at corners, sized for passport/phone/sunglasses
- Angular collar shape based on vintage safari jackets, frames the face
- Very light canvassing
- Larger buttons to balance the jacket's front
- Centre button creates V-shape silhouette when fastened
- Available in: Natural Irish Linen (heavier weight, reduces creasing), Brown Cotton Panama (structured, open weave for humidity)
- Made by Ascot Chang
- Made-to-order program available in thousands of fabrics
