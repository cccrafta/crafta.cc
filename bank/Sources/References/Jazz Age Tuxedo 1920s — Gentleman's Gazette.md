## Summary
Comprehensive history of the tuxedo in the 1920s. The Prince of Wales elevated the dinner jacket over the tailcoat. Championed midnight blue. Vogue lamented the "decline of masculine elegance."

## Key Claims
- Prince of Wales regularly chose dinner jacket over tailcoat, elevating it to standard
- Championed midnight blue — appeared richer under artificial light
- Vogue article lamented decline of masculine elegance
- American style followed British trends
