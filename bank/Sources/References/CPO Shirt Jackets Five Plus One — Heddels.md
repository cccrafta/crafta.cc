## Summary
2017 roundup of six CPO shirt makers with specific fabric weights, construction details, and prices. The most useful weight comparison data for contemporary makers from that era.

## Key Claims
- Schott CPO Jacket: 24 oz. melton wool (olive version); 20 oz. wool (black version); $195; corozo buttons (olive) or anchor-embossed buttons (black)
- Nigel Cabourn x Lybro CPO: heavy-duty wool, elbow patches, bartacking at stress points, custom Lybro metal buttons; $280
- Iron Heart Ultra-Heavy CPO: two-tone twill with darker weft, chainstitch runoff, felled seams, custom copper buttons, front hand pockets; $400
- Buzz Rickson CPO: single chest pocket, pointed collar, historically accurate 1st-model reproduction; $200
- Fidelity CPO: 22 oz. melton wool, two chest pockets, two front pockets, triple stitching, naval-themed buttons; $279
- Engineered Garments CPO: two different grey herringbone fabrics, three differently-sized chest pockets, snap closure, hidden pockets, two-snap throat strap; $247
