## Summary
Practical buying guide for henleys organized by style tier (refined, athletic, heritage, budget). Recommends pairing with workwear outerwear.

## Key Claims
- Heritage tier: The Real McCoys, Iron Heart best for pairing with leather jackets and field coats
- Merz b. Schwanen for loopwheeled quality
- Indigo-dyed options: Orslow, RRL, Pure Blue Japan, Kapital
- Budget under $50: Uniqlo, Todd Snyder, J. Crew, Pistol Lake
