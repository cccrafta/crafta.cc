## Summary
Preview of AW Sylvester's book on workwear, featured on Permanent Style. Examines how workwear has moved "from utility to subculture to luxury fashion and back again" and why denim holds cultural power beyond its practical properties.

## Key Claims
- Workwear has gone "from utility to subculture to luxury fashion and back again"
- Denim is "not the most practical of materials" but "holds so much cultural history in its warp and weft"
- The book traces workwear's journey through multiple cultural phases — not a linear progression
- Author is AW Sylvester, book title: "An Informal Guide to Workwear"
