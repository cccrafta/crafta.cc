## Summary
Denim consultant Anne Oudard's FW26-27 forecast, presented in two chapters: "Working Craft" and "Blooming Darkness." Working Craft positions denim as rooted in authenticity and precision while introducing adaptive performance, smart fibers, and fully traceable cotton. The forecast signals a convergence of luxury materials with workwear silhouettes.

## Key Claims
- FW26-27 forecast structured as two themes: "Working Craft" and "Blooming Darkness"
- Working Craft introduces premium wool blends with 60% cashmere and gray denims with wool and organic cotton bases
- Viscose pleated denim allows "beautiful movement" — tailoring influence on workwear fabric
- Flannels and tartans within denim collections nod to classic tailoring
- Thermolite fabrics add warmth without bulkiness for modern workwear
- Sustainable elastic alternatives enhance freedom of movement
- Rare organic green cotton eliminates need for sulfur bottoms, overdyes, or tints
- Cotton-rich stories highlight mills' sustainable cotton initiatives and community uplift
- Couture "seamlessly merges with denim" in this direction
- Fully traceable cotton stories are a key component
