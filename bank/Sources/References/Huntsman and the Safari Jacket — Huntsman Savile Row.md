## Summary
Huntsman's relationship with the safari suit traces to the golden age of Hollywood. They made bespoke safari suits for Clark Gable, Ava Gardner, and Grace Kelly for the 1953 film *Mogambo*.

## Key Claims
- Huntsman crafted bespoke safari suits for Clark Gable, Ava Gardner, and Grace Kelly for *Mogambo* (1953)
- Creative Director Campbell Carey designed current collection drawing on heritage with contemporary sensibilities
- Current offerings include herringbone and navy seersucker safari jackets
- Ready-to-wear Seagreen Linen Herringbone Safari Jacket uses softer construction: thinner shoulder pads, lighter canvassing, soft sleeve head
