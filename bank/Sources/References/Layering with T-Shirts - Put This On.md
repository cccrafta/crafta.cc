## Summary
Practical guide on layering t-shirts under jackets and shirts. Covers neckline choices, length considerations, and when collars vs crew necks work.

## Key Claims
- Thinnest layers closest to body, bulk increases outward
- Structured jackets pair better with formal shirts; soft/unstructured jackets work with tees
- Getting a collar (via jacket or open shirt layer) frames the face
- T-shirt length shouldn't reach jacket hem
