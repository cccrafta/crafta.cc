## Summary
2025 Heddels coverage of Cockpit USA launching what they describe as one of the few authentically made-in-USA CPO shirts. Notes the gap in domestically manufactured CPOs despite the garment's American military origins.

## Key Claims
- Fabric: 85/15% wool/nylon melton cloth, 20-22 oz. per square yard
- Made in USA; available in navy and olive
- Design features: two front chest pockets, anchor buttons, curved hem, silk-lined yoke and collar, removable metal Chief Petty Officer anchor pin
- Priced at $325
- Article notes "few, if any, brands are making authentic CPO shirts in the USA" — suggesting the made-in-America version is notable in the current market
- Described as "a three-season beast" — boxy enough to layer over heavyweight knit in winter, refined enough for cool spring
