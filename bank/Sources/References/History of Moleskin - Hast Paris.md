## Summary
French perspective on moleskin history. Covers the fabric's role in French industrial workwear — steel workers in particular.

## Key Claims
- 19th century: moleskin used in France by steel workers — resistant to molten metal splashes
- Used in Sheffield, England for steel manufacturers
- Water-repellent character
- French brands Le Laboureur, Le Mont St. Michel still operating

## Notes
- New French source — valuable for perspective from the country where moleskin workwear originated
