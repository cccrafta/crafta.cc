## Summary
Canvas ETC overview of duck canvas fabric history, weight classifications, and commercial applications.

## Key Claims
- Mid-1800s: mills like Mount Vernon Mill in Maryland increased duck canvas production
- Duck canvas originally used for ship sails and tents
- By treating with wax, duck fabric becomes waterproof (waxed cotton)
- Starts stiffer but softens with wear; smooth surface makes it less prone to snagging
- Carhartt and Pointer Brand use duck canvas for chore jackets, pants, and overalls
