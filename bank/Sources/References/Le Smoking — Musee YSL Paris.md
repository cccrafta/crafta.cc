## Summary
Official museum account of Le Smoking's creation. Part of fall/winter 1966 "Pop Art" collection. Inspired by Marlene Dietrich photograph from 1933. Only one haute couture version sold initially — the Rive Gauche ready-to-wear version was the commercial success.

## Key Claims
- Le Smoking debuted August 1966 in "Pop Art" collection
- Inspired by Marlene Dietrich photograph (1933)
- YSL quote: "A woman dressed as a man must be at the height of femininity"
- Only one haute couture version sold; Rive Gauche version was the success
- Saint Laurent designed over 200 variations until retirement in 2002
