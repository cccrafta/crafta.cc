## Summary
Overview of how major denim mills are responding to the heritage-luxury convergence for FW26-27, balancing nostalgia with innovation and sustainability. Each mill interprets the trend differently but converges on vintage textures with modern wearability.

## Key Claims
- Candiani Denim sees a shift toward "the essence of jeans when they were truly meaningful, reflecting rich heritage"
- Calik Denim's "Roots Age" concept inspired by the gold rush: ultra-nappy textures with contemporary wearability
- Calik's "Worktive" range offers fashionable durable workwear-inspired pieces
- Evlox follows "sanctuary dressing" — macro trend rooted in vintage aesthetics, self-care, emotional comfort
- Bossa's "Future Relics" collection blends nostalgia, innovation, and sustainability
- Bossa draws from vintage textures and rugged bull denims with modern surface treatments
- Cautious mood overshadowed the start of FW26-27 season — suppliers refining for more conservative buys
- Authenticity, comfort, and luxury guiding trend directions
