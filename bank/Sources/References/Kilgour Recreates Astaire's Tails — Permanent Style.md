## Summary
Kilgour French & Stanbury made Astaire's Top Hat tailcoat. Cutter Del Smith attempted to recreate it, discovering technical details for dance: large sleevehead, exaggerated rope shoulder for arm movement, rounded waistcoat edges to prevent snagging on raised legs.

## Key Claims
- Kilgour made the original Top Hat suit
- Enlarged sleevehead and shoulder rope accommodated dance movement
- Waistcoat edges rounded rather than pointed for leg freedom
