## Worth Watching

**1. "Working Craft" — Heritage denim goes high-end.** Multiple mills (Candiani, Calik, Bossa, Evlox) are converging on the same idea: vintage textures and workwear silhouettes, but with premium materials (cashmere blends, wool-organic cotton, viscose pleated denim). This isn't just nostalgic — it's the denim industry repositioning heritage as a luxury proposition. Worth tracking how brands downstream interpret this.

**2. Resoling and repair as editorial content.** Crown Northampton publishing their resole process, Archival Clothing breaking down vintage Filson construction — repair and longevity are becoming content strategies, not just service offerings. Crafta could own this angle harder.
